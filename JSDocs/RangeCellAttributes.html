Class: RangeCellAttributes

RangeCellAttributes(rng)

Collect all the relevant cell attributes of a Google Sheets range into a data structure for use in other classes. Loops over all the cells in the range and builds data structures of the attributes of each cell. Merged ranges are treated differently from attributes that apply too single cells and aremapped to HTML rowspan and colspan attributes.
